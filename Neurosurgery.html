Neurology Services at UNO Superspeciality Clinic

At UNO Superspeciality Clinic, our Neurology Services stand at the forefront of comprehensive care for neurological disorders. Led by a team of esteemed neurologists, our department is dedicated to diagnosing, treating, and managing a wide spectrum of neurological conditions with precision and compassion.

Expert Care for Neurological Conditions


Our neurology specialists bring a wealth of experience and expertise in managing various neurological disorders, including but not limited to:

  • Stroke Care: Immediate and comprehensive care for stroke patients, including acute interventions and rehabilitation programs to aid recovery.
  • Epilepsy Management:Personalised treatment plans and innovative therapies for individuals living with epilepsy to achieve better seizure control and improved quality of life.
  • Multiple Sclerosis (MS):Advanced diagnostics and treatment options to manage MS symptoms and slow disease progression
  • Diagnosis and tailored management plans for chronic headaches and migraines.
  • Specialised care for conditions like Parkinson's disease, essential tremor, and dystonia, focusing on symptom management and enhancing daily living.

State-of-the-Art Diagnostics and Treatments


Our neurology department is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools and cutting-edge technology, allowing for precise assessments and accurate diagnoses. We offer a range of innovative treatments, including medication management, interventional procedures, neurorehabilitation, and access to clinical trials for novel therapies.

Compassionate, Patient-Centred Approach


At UNO, we prioritise a patient-centric approach. We understand the challenges faced by individuals with neurological conditions and their families. Our team not only focuses on medical intervention but also provides comprehensive support, education, and guidance to empower patients in their journey towards better health.

Partnering for Better Neurological Health


Collaboration is at the core of our approach. We work closely with other specialties within our clinic, ensuring that patients with complex medical needs receive integrated, multidisciplinary care for optimal outcomes.

Experience Excellence in Neurological Care


At UNO Superspeciality Clinic, our Neurology Services combine expertise, innovation, and empathy to provide exceptional care for individuals living with neurological conditions. We are dedicated to improving lives and fostering hope for a brighter, healthier future.
