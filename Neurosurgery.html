Neurosurgery Services at UNO Superspeciality Clinic

Embark on a journey of advanced neurosurgical care at UNO Superspeciality Clinic, where our Neurosurgery Services stand as a beacon of excellence in the comprehensive treatment of neurosurgical disorders. Guided by a team of distinguished neurosurgeons, our department is committed to delivering precise, compassionate, and cutting-edge care for a wide range of neurosurgical conditions.

Expert Surgical Care for Neurological Disorders


Our neurosurgical specialists bring forth a wealth of experience and proficiency in managing various neurological disorders, showcasing expertise in:

  • Brain Tumor Surgery: Comprehensive and personalized treatment plans for the surgical management of brain tumors, with a focus on optimal tumor resection and post-operative care.
  • Spinal Surgery: Specialized interventions for spinal conditions, including disc herniation, spinal stenosis, and spinal deformities, with an emphasis on restoring function and relieving pain.
  • Neurovascular Surgery: Advanced procedures for treating conditions like aneurysms and arteriovenous malformations, ensuring timely and effective intervention to prevent complications.
  • Peripheral Nerve Surgery: Surgical expertise in addressing peripheral nerve disorders, such as carpal tunnel syndrome and peripheral nerve injuries, with a focus on functional restoration.
  • Trauma and Emergency Neurosurgery: Immediate and comprehensive care for neurosurgical emergencies, including traumatic brain injuries and spinal cord injuries.

State-of-the-Art Diagnostics and Surgical Interventions


Equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and cutting-edge surgical technology, our neurosurgery department enables precise assessments and accurate surgical interventions. We offer a spectrum of innovative treatments, ranging from minimally invasive procedures to complex neurosurgical interventions, ensuring the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Compassionate, Patient-Centred Approach


At UNO, we prioritize a patient-centric approach, understanding the unique challenges faced by individuals undergoing neurosurgical procedures and their families. Our team not only focuses on the surgical intervention itself but also provides comprehensive support, education, and guidance to empower patients throughout their journey towards recovery.

Partnering for Better Neurological Health


Collaboration is integral to our approach. We work closely with other specialties within our clinic, ensuring that patients with complex medical needs receive integrated, multidisciplinary care for optimal outcomes. Our commitment to teamwork extends to post-operative rehabilitation, ensuring a seamless continuum of care.
