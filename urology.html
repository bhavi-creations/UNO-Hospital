Urology Services at UNO Superspeciality Clinic

Discover excellence in urological care at UNO Superspeciality Clinic, where our Urology Services are designed to address a comprehensive range of urological conditions. Our esteemed team of urologists is committed to delivering precise diagnosis, advanced treatments, and compassionate management for patients seeking urological care.

Expert Care for Urological Conditions


Our urology specialists bring a wealth of experience in managing various urological disorders, providing personalized care for conditions including, but not limited to:

  • Prostate Health: Comprehensive evaluation and management of prostate conditions, offering advanced treatments for prostate enlargement, prostatitis, and prostate cancer.
  • Bladder Concerns: Specialized care for conditions affecting the bladder, such as urinary incontinence, bladder infections, and interstitial cystitis.
  • Male Infertility: Evaluation and treatment of male reproductive health issues, including infertility, erectile dysfunction, and testosterone imbalances.
  • Female Urology: Dedicated care for women's urological health, addressing issues such as urinary tract infections, pelvic organ prolapse, and incontinence.

State-of-the-Art Diagnostics and Treatments


Our urology department is equipped with cutting-edge diagnostic tools and advanced technology for precise assessments and accurate diagnoses. We offer innovative treatments, including minimally invasive surgeries, laser therapies, and medical management, ensuring comprehensive and effective care for our patients.

Compassionate, Patient-Centred Approach


At UNO, we understand the impact urological conditions can have on individuals and their families. Our patient-centric approach goes beyond medical intervention, providing comprehensive support, education, and guidance to empower patients in their journey toward urological health.

Partnering for Better Urological Health


Collaboration is fundamental to our approach. We work closely with other specialties within our clinic to ensure that patients with complex medical needs receive integrated, multidisciplinary care for optimal outcomes.

Experience Excellence in Urological Care


UNO Superspeciality Clinic's Urology Services combine expertise, innovation, and empathy to provide exceptional care for individuals facing urological conditions. We are dedicated to improving lives and fostering hope for a healthier future.
